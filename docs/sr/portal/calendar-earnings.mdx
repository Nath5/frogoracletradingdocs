# Economic Calendar / Earnings

The FrogOracle Trading Portal provides integrated access to economic calendar events and upcoming earnings releases to help you stay informed about market-moving events.

## Economic Calendar Panel

The Economic Calendar panel displays upcoming economic events and data releases that may impact market movements.

### Accessing the Economic Calendar

1. Load a chart with a valid ticker symbol
2. Click the menu icon in the top-right corner to open the sidebar
3. Select the calendar icon from the sidebar to open the Economic Calendar panel

![image](_media/ui/calendar.png ':size=600')

### Calendar Features

The Economic Calendar panel displays:

- **Event Name**: The name of the economic event or data release
- **Date and Time**: When the event is scheduled to occur
- **Country**: Which country the event is for
- **Period**: Which period the data release represents
- **Est. Act. Prev**: Estimated value, actual value, previous value

## Upcoming Earnings Panel

The Upcoming Earnings panel shows scheduled earnings releases for companies, helping you anticipate potential volatility and significant price movements.

### Accessing Upcoming Earnings

1. Load a chart with a valid ticker symbol
2. Click the menu icon in the top-right corner to open the sidebar
3. Select the earnings icon from the sidebar to open the Upcoming Earnings panel

![image](_media/ui/earnings.png ':size=600')

### Earnings Information

The Upcoming Earnings panel displays:

- **Company and Ticker**: The company name and stock symbol
- **Earnings Date**: When the earnings report is scheduled
- **Expected vs. Actual**: Earnings estimates and actual results (when available)
- **Pre-market vs. After-market**: Indicates whether earnings will be released before market open or after market close

## Integration with Chart Analysis

Economic events and earnings releases can significantly impact price movements:

### Economic Calendar Impact

- Monitor scheduled events that may affect the overall market or specific sectors
- Plan trades around high-impact events to avoid unexpected volatility
- Use the calendar to understand market reactions to data releases

### Earnings Release Impact

- Anticipate increased volatility around earnings dates
- Watch for price movements leading up to earnings (positioning)
- Consider pre-market vs. after-market timing when planning trades
- Review historical patterns of how the stock reacts to earnings

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues with these features:

- **No Data Available**: Calendar and earnings data requires an active internet connection
- **Loading Issues**: If data doesn't load, try refreshing the page
- **Missing Events**: Some smaller companies may have limited earnings coverage
